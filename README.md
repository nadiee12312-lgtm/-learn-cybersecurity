## ⚖️ The golden rule / La regla de oro

> **Only practice on what is YOURS or what you have PERMISSION to test.**
> **Solo practica en lo que es TUYO o en lo que tienes PERMISO de probar.**

Everything here is for **learning defense and authorized testing** (your own lab, legal practice platforms like TryHackMe/HackTheBox/DVWA, or clients who hired you in writing). Accessing systems without permission is a **crime**. Knowledge isn't the crime; **unauthorized use** is. Be one of the good ones.
