## ⚖️ Before you start — the golden rule

> **Only practice on what is YOURS or what you have PERMISSION to test.**

Everything this academy teaches is for **learning to defend and for authorized testing** (your own lab, legal practice platforms like TryHackMe/HackTheBox/DVWA, or clients who hired you in writing). Accessing systems without permission is a **crime** — in the US (CFAA) and most countries there's real jail time. Knowledge isn't the crime; **unauthorized use** is. Be one of the good ones.

## 📖 How to use this academy

1. Start at your level (know nothing → "Your first day").
2. Read a book, **practice what you learn** on a legal platform.
3. Don't rush. 30 minutes a day > 5 hours at once.
4. Don't quit at the first frustration — that IS the job.
